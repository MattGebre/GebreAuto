Frequently Asked Questions

QUESTIONS AND CONCERNS

  • Can I return a product if it is damaged or if I don't want it anymore?

    • Yes. You can return a product as long as you return it within 30 days of the transaction. If a product is damaged we will now replace it for you for free!

  • How do I track my order?

    • After ordering a product, we will send you a link to the package tracker automatically via your email address.

  • What do I do if a part does not fit properly?

    • If you are having fitment issues with your purchase, you can contact us here. Our technical staff will gladly assist you in the installation of your product.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    • You can call our customer service department at 647-133-7020 if you want to make adjustments to your order.

  • Can I ship my order to my house and still have an installation set up?

    • Yes, we will ship directly to your house if you prefer to bring the tires to your appointment yourself. Just select the ship to home option at checkout.
